Who Am I?

I am a UT Austin senior, of 2017, graduating with a degree in Management Information Systems and an Elements of Computer Science Certificate. Other than playing video games or participating in a wide variety of sports, I enjoy taking the time to build certain projects for classes and have begun entering the world of white hat (ethical) hacking.

Striving to push my abilities as a programmer and web developer, I have been looking into numerous types of coding languages and concepts. Being exposed to different varities of programming (Python, Linux, HTML, Terminal Codes) I enjoy learning what each can do and how they can fit with each other..

While I am currently looking for a full-time position for after graduation (Spring 2017), I have completed internships for the past 2 summers at IGT and Q1Media; a multinational company and an advertising startup, respectively. Within each, I have seen the Project Management aspect of product development and the Business Intelligence perspective within a company and enjoy being able to be a part of the process in developing a product while being able to track the data that goes along with shipping a project.

In the future, I hope to learn how to create smartwatch applications as well as using Raspberry Pi's to create a new development in the "smart home" trend (perhaps a smart mirror?).
